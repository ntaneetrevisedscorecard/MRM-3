About Us

Mata Rajrani Memorial Hospital

Established with a vision to provide affordable, quality healthcare to Ambikapur and the surrounding tribal and rural communities of Chhattisgarh.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive, patient-centered medical services using modern technology while keeping healthcare accessible and affordable for every family.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare institution in Central India, recognized for clinical excellence, innovation, and compassionate care that transforms lives.

Infrastructure

24/7 Emergency, fully-equipped ICU, Neonatal ICU, modern Operation Theaters, in-house Pathology Lab, Pharmacy, and ample parking.

Our Services

Departments & Specialties

We offer a wide range of medical services under one roof, staffed by experienced specialists.

General Medicine

Chronic disease management and preventive health check-ups.

Pediatrics

Expert child healthcare from newborn to adolescent.

Orthopedics

Fracture care, joint replacement, sports injuries.

Gynecology

Prenatal care, deliveries, and gynecological surgeries.

Cardiology

Heart diagnostics, ECG, and preventive cardiac care.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation, post-surgical recovery, pain management.

ENT

Ear, Nose & Throat care including microsurgeries.

24/7 Ambulance

Emergency ambulance covering Ambikapur region.
